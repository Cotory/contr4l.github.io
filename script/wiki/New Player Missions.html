New to Torn?

We understand that a game of this size can be overwhelming. We want to help you get started in your journey with us.

Start your Journey here!

We currently provide Missions just for new players . The Missions are here to guide you and explain the in's and out's of basic day to day game play. We will give you a step by step guide as to how to complete these missions and hopefully this will help you get well on your way in our big city. So let's begin! Be sure to click each Mission title for a detailed explanation of how to accomplish those goals.

Get a job
Mission steps
1. Click the newspaper link on the left hand menu.
2. At the top of the newspaper, click the "Job Listings" section.
3. Choose a job from the "Starter Jobs" category.
4. Once you have chosen your job, click the "Go to Interview" link directly beside of it.
5. You will see a dialogue, with the option to sign up. If this is the job for you click "Sign up". If not click back and explore the other Starter Jobs.
6. Once you have signed up, you will be redirected to your employment page.
Mission description
Ya'll need to earn yourself a pay packet bucko if you gonna survive here. Even those fancy pants Corleone's started out hawking salad dressing, so you'd better try looking in the local newspaper, under the Job Listings page. You ain't much yet so you can't afford to be too picky. Heck I still shine shoes for $4 a pop. When you get a fancy pair give me a call. Jobs also build up your stats every day, and the higher your stats the better jobs come-a-callin' for you. So get out there and make yourself some dough.
Start an education course
Mission steps
1. Click the education link on the left hand menu.
2. Click on the education area you would like to take a course in. There are 12 different areas to choose from on the education page.
3. Once you have chosen your area of study, choose which class from the list you wish to enter.
4. At the bottom of the course description you will see "Join this course". If this is the one you want click on Join this course.
5. After clicking Join this course, you will see a page describing how long the course takes to complete and the cost. If satisfied, click "Yes sign me up".
6. You will then be redirected to a page telling you, you have signed up and the length of time until you have completed the course.
Mission description

To move on to big league hustles you gotta first do your time in the petty crime grind. Searching the streets for cash is simple, plus you won't get busted doing it. So get walking the streets and earn yourself some eats. $25 should do it.

1. Navigate to the awards section in the left bar.
2. Here you can see all possible honour bars and medals you could earn. Try and earn one!
Mission description
In Torn, Medals and Honors are given for doing stuff really well. I ain't never got an award, except one that said I was a "potential fire hazard". I guess that's something! Oh I also won most handsome horse once. Even though I ain't a horse! You can check your progress on the 'Awards' page: use the link on the sidebar.
Level up to level 2
Mission steps
1. Leveling can be obtained through attacking and selecting the "Leave" option, and by doing crimes and succeeding. Attacking helps you gain levels faster if you "Leave" your opponent.
2. Once you have reached enough experience to gain a level you will see NOTE! Congratulations! You have enough experience to go up to level 2! Click here to upgrade! at the top of your homepage. You will also see Level: 2 [upgrade] next to your level on the left side of your screen.
3. Click either of those links to upgrade your level.
4. When you have clicked the upgrade you will see the message Congratulations! You upgraded your level to 2! This means you have successfully upgraded to the next level.
Mission description
To do this you gotta reach level 2. Most of the stuff you'll do in Torn will give you experience and level you up. And levelling up will give you access to more areas of the city and increase your life. That's why I ain't got much and I look like crap, I just sit around all day. Did you know I'm only 25?

Post Missions

Once you have finished your new player missions, you will get a small goodbye message from George to encourage you to continue playing, and set you on your way to greatness!

Great Work new player. I'm proud of you!

It took you 26 weeks and 3 days to complete all my missions. That's a pretty good time for someone as green around the gills as you. I got high hopes for you new player. Don't make the same mistakes old George did.

Now you know the basics - it's time to move on.

You'll need to learn quick, think fast and stay alert. The competition around here can get pretty intense so make sure you train hard in the gym, get educated quickly, get promoted at work and always be on guard. Oh and don't leave your breakfast burrito out in the sun too long. Learnt that the hard way.

I'm sure a good looking guy like you will attract attention and it won't be long before you get a marriage proposal. I might even propose myself! But look, getting hitched is good and all, you get plenty of benefits, just don't lose focus new player. There's still plenty of missions, crimes and wars to be won.

You're on your own now and life will start to get harder. Make sure you pace yourself... Torn is a long-term endeavour and gets more exciting the longer you're here.

If you overdo it, you'll need to wait for your energy to regenerate naturally. To speed this up you could buy a donator pack for increased energy levels and quicker recovery time.

Good Luck my friend. And if you ever see someone who looks like your pal George lying in the gutter, do me a favour and whizz in the other direction. See ya around new player.
